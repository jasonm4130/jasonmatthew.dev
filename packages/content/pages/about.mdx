I'm Jason Matthew, an Engineering Manager based in Brisbane, Australia. I lead three AI teams and a Search team at [Squiz](https://www.squiz.net) — 30+ engineers building AI platforms, content intelligence, conversational search, and digital asset management. I'm also still one of the top code contributors on the projects I lead, because I believe you can't lead what you don't understand.

## Lead by Example

I don't think you have to choose between leading and building. The best engineering leaders I've worked with stay close to the craft — not as a hobby, but because it makes every other part of the job better. You give better code reviews when you're writing code. You make better architecture calls when you've felt the pain of a bad abstraction. You earn more trust when the team sees you in the trenches with them.

By night I ship side projects — SaaS products, algorithm visualizers, tools that scratch an itch. Not because I have to, but because building things is how I think.
