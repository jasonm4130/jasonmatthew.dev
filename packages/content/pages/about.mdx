I'm Jason Matthew, an engineer based in Brisbane, Australia. I build AI systems and cloud infrastructure at scale at [Squiz](https://www.squiz.net) — production RAG pipelines, ML inference platforms, and edge-native search products used by world-class universities and global brands. I also lead the engineering org behind them: 30+ engineers across AI, Search, and Platform squads. I still write about 45% of the code across our core repositories, because I don't think you can make good architecture calls without feeling the pain of your own abstractions.

## Building Is How I Think

My background spans a deliberate loop through Tech Lead, acting Product Manager, and Engineering Manager. I chose the EM path to learn how to drive change beyond my own squad — and I did. But I drive the most value when I'm deep in the code, setting the architecture, and building the tools that move the needle.

By night I ship side projects — SaaS products, algorithm visualizers, tools that scratch an itch. Not because I have to, but because building things is how I think.
