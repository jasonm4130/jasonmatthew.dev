## The Problem with Single-Tool AI Workflows

## The Three Tiers

### Tier 1: Repomix — Structural Mapping

{/* npx repomix --style xml --output-show-stats */}
{/* Package your repo blueprints into a digestible format */}
{/* This is the "local state" capture */}

### Tier 2: Gemini — Research & Planning

{/* Upload the Repomix XML to standard Gemini chat */}
{/* Have it analyze architecture, identify gaps, spot unknown unknowns */}
{/* Draft a targeted Deep Research prompt */}
{/* Then run Deep Research for live-web patterns (S3 Vectors, Durable Lambda, etc.) */}
{/* Output: a spec.md combining local context with modern patterns */}

### Tier 3: Cursor — Surgical Implementation

{/* Open spec.md, use @File and @Symbol mentions */}
{/* New chat session per task — session hygiene */}
{/* Zero credits spent on planning or research */}
